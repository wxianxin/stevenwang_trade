Semiconductor Manufacturing International Corporation

SMIC SMIC is a nation's will

20161031

SMIC is a semi-conductor company based in Shanghai, China. It is one of the most prospective silicon factory in China and its shareholders include FGW, an SOI of Chinese government. It represents the best industrial technology effort in China.

Logic and memory chips are the most imported products for China, even more than crude oil. Over one Trillion CNY(￥) chips are imported every year and the number is still increasing rapidly.

It is worth mentioning that, South Korea and Taiwan are the two major regions that produce silicon-based products, namely TSMC and Samsung. If you happen to know a little about the geopolitical situation of eastern Asia, you will see the gravity here. So, semi-conductor industry in China, represented by SMIC, is more than just a public company, it's more like a nation‘s will.
